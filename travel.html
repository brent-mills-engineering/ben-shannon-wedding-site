Getting here

Baltimore/Washington International (BWI) is the closest airport, about a 20-minute drive from downtown. Amtrak and MARC trains stop at Baltimore Penn Station, a short ride from both venues.

Coming by car? Both the ceremony and reception are in Mount Vernon. We've pulled together parking options and day-of directions on their own page.
