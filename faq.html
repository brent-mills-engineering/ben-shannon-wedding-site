Where should we stay?

We're holding room blocks at the Courtyard by Marriott (Inner Harbor) and Delta Hotels Baltimore North under the Frede-Groff Wedding. Reserve by September 2, 2026 for the group rate.

What is the schedule for the day?

The ceremony begins at 3:00 PM, with the reception, dinner, and dancing to follow at 4:00 PM. The full run of the day is on the Schedule page.

Where is the venue?

The ceremony is at St. Ignatius Church (740 N. Calvert St) and the reception is at the Maryland Center for History & Culture (610 Park Ave), both in Mount Vernon and a short walk apart. See the Schedule for maps and directions.

Where can we park?

Both venues are in Mount Vernon, with garages and street parking nearby. Parking spots and day-of directions live on the Parking page.

How can we get there?

Baltimore/Washington International (BWI) is the nearest airport, about 20 minutes away, and Amtrak stops at Penn Station. Driving and parking details are on Travel & Stay and Parking.
